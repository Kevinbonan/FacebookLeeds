# Client Requirements Checklist

## Business

- Confirm the exact offer or service name to include in the WhatsApp template.
- Confirm the final WhatsApp group invitation link.
- Confirm who owns ongoing monitoring and support.
- Confirm whether the client wants only invitation delivery tracking or also downstream conversion tracking.

## Meta / Facebook

- Provide access to the Facebook Page that owns the lead form.
- Provide access to the Meta app or authorize a setup session.
- Confirm the exact Facebook lead form name or names.
- Confirm the phone field exists on the form.
- Confirm the consent field exists on the form and share its exact label and field name if available.

## WhatsApp

- Provide access to the WhatsApp Business Account.
- Confirm the sending phone number to use.
- Approve the final template message copy.
- Submit and approve the WhatsApp template in Meta before go-live.

## Compliance

- Confirm the privacy policy URL.
- Confirm the lead form consent language covers WhatsApp contact.
- Confirm the business is allowed to send this invitation in the target jurisdiction.

## Operations

- Confirm who receives error alerts.
- Confirm where lead logs should be stored:
  - local database
  - Google Sheets
  - managed production database
- Confirm the deployment environment:
  - VPS
  - Render
  - Railway
  - Heroku-style platform
  - internal server
