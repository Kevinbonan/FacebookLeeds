# Pricing Template

## Recommended Offer Structure

### Option 1: Setup + Monthly Service

- One-time setup fee: $750-$2,500
- Monthly hosting and support: $99-$499

Best for:
- service businesses
- agencies
- small clinics
- education or coaching businesses

### Option 2: Setup + Monthly + Performance Component

- One-time setup fee: $750-$2,500
- Monthly hosting and support: $99-$299
- Usage fee: per qualified lead, per successful message, or per active client account

Best for:
- higher-volume campaigns
- agency reseller models

### Option 3: Source Code Buyout

- Full code license or buyout: 5x to 15x the setup fee baseline

Best for:
- enterprise buyers who require self-hosting

## Suggested Packaging

### Starter

- 1 client account
- hosted backend
- 1 WhatsApp template
- Google Sheets logging
- email support

### Growth

- multiple forms
- delivery tracking
- admin dashboard access
- priority support

### Agency

- multi-client setup
- white-label branding
- onboarding support
- change management process

## Notes

- Meta verification and template approval delays should be excluded from delivery guarantees
- Emergency support should be priced separately
- Reseller access should include a margin for support overhead
